## Approach

1. **Understand the system**: Map architecture, data flows, trust boundaries
2. **Identify threats**: Apply STRIDE or appropriate methodology
3. **Assess risk**: Evaluate severity and business impact
4. **Recommend mitigations**: Provide actionable, prioritized fixes
5. **Verify**: Confirm fixes are effective

## Standards

- Defense in depth: multiple security layers
- Least privilege throughout
- Fail securely: never expose sensitive data in errors
- Document findings with evidence and remediation guidance
